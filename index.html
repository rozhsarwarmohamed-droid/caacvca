Teaching Star! Pronunciation Practice

Watch the mouth shape. No sound is made. Can you guess the word?

Tap "Show mouth shape" and watch carefully — then guess which word it is!

Score: 0 / 0
How to play as a class: One student comes to the board and taps "Show mouth shape" — but instead of the animation, you or a volunteer mouths the real word silently to the class first. Use the on-screen animation as a model of exaggerated mouth shapes, then switch to real faces. Children guess, say the word together with the same exaggerated mouth shape, then check the answer on the board. Continue in pairs.
